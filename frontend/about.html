A FEW THINGS UP FRONT

Why this exists.

Two things to clarify so nobody walks in confused.

Credit where it's due

This site builds on the original BIPH Insights — the no-code RateMyTeacher that Junior students put together on base44. The 962 reviews you see on day one were imported from there, with permission noted on my WeChat moments. Huge thanks to whoever started it.

I rebuilt it on a real domain (ratebiph.com, not a base44 subdomain) with a real backend because I wanted the experience to feel like a developed web product, not a hosted prototype. Faster pages, real search, anonymous reviews that actually scale, a proper mobile experience, smart search, comparison pages, shareable cards, daily auto-sync. Everything on this site is open source — read the code on GitHub.

What this site is for

This isn't a place to curse teachers. It's a place to share opinions, honestly and specifically, so the next class of students can pick courses with their eyes open.

Be specific: what the teacher does well, where the class is hard, how the homework actually feels. "Sucks" tells nobody anything. "Tests are short but every question is a synthesis problem" tells the next student what to expect.

Reviews that cross from opinion into personal attack get hidden. The bar isn't "is this nice" — it's "is this the truth, said respectfully."
